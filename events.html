How Things work TL Talks

How things work is low prep event in which tinkerers meet at a place and break open objects that are not working and understand the parts involved in making and working of these things. These are community driven events and are a huge success. Following are a few of these sessions held in the past:

  • Hard disk
  • 3D Printer
  • Laptop
  • LCD TV
  • Projector
  • Accelerometer

    • And many more....

    ABOUT THE TL LECTURE SERIES

    ‘TL Talks’ have begun with the purpose of taking ahead the very spirit of innovation, by acquainting students with great technologies and the brains behind them, to inspire students to find the inventor within them.
